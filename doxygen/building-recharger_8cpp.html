void building_recharge ( player_type *  player_ptr)

魔道具の使用回数を回復させる施設のメインルーチン / Recharge rods, wands and staffs

The player can select the number of charges to add
(up to a limit), and the recharge never fails.

The cost for rods depends on the level of the rod. The prices
for recharging wands and staffs are dependent on the cost of
